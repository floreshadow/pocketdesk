PocketDesk

Connect your iPhone to a monitor to get a full desktop experience.

How to connect

1
Get a Lightning → HDMI or USB-C → HDMI adapter ($10–$20)
2
Plug your iPhone into the adapter, connect HDMI to your monitor
3
Open Safari on your iPhone and visit this page — the desktop launches on your monitor
4
Pair a Bluetooth keyboard & mouse to your iPhone via Settings → Bluetooth
* Full external display detection is automatic when connected
